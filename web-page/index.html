Welcome to the Statistics of the Tertiary Education in Europe

Explore the trends and distributions of students enrolled in tertiary education across the Europe. Our interactive visualizations provide insights into various aspects of tertiary education, such as gender distribution, geographical differences, trends over time, and the breakdown by education sector and level. We will use the data

Overview of the data

Stacked Barchart: Gender Distribution by Country

This barchart shows the distribution of students enrolled in tertiary education across different countries. Bars are sorted based on the number of enrolled students. Each bar is divided between male and female students, as indicated in the legend.

Use the dropdown list to select different years and observe how the distribution changes over time. Hover over the bars to see the exact amount of students.

Choropleth Map: Geographic Distribution of Students

The map highlights the distribution of students enrolled in tertiary education across different countries. Countries are shaded based on the number of students enrolled.

Adjust the year using the slider to see how the distribution of students changes over time. Hover over the bars to see the exact amount of students (in thousands).

Deeper in details

Sankey Diagram: Distribution by Level of Education

This diagram visualizes the flow of students through different levels of education, providing insights into how students progress within the tertiary education system. Here we used Netherlands as an example.

Hover over the nodes and the links to see the exact percentage of students enrolled in each level of education.

Timeline

Changes in Total Number of Students by Country

This timeline graph shows the trend in the total number of students enrolled in tertiary education for a selected country.

Use the dropdown list to select different countries and observe how the distribution changes over time. Mind that the scale on the vertical axis changes as well. Hover over the circles to see the exact amount of students at each timepoint.

Education Sector

Pie Chart: Distribution by Education Sector within a Country

The pie chart breaks down the student population within a selected country by different sectors of education for a particular year.

Use the dropdown list to select different countries. Use the slider to select the year to update the chart and see the distribution of students across various education sectors. Hover over the chart to see the exact amount of students at each timepoint.
